In its history , it was the capital three times: of the Western Roman Empire (402-476), [ 5 ] of the Ostrogothic Kingdom (493-540) and of the Byzantine Exarchate (584-751). For the vestiges of this bright past, the complex of the first Christian monuments of Ravenna has been included, since 1996 , in the list of Italian World Heritage Sites by UNESCO , as a serial site " early Christian monuments of Ravenna ".

The city center is located at8 km from the Adriatic Sea , to which it is connected by the Candiano Canal . Urban offshoots extend to the sea through the Port of Ravenna . Ravenna's beaches are called "lidi" and are administratively classified as hamlets. The nine Ravenna lidi are defined, based on their position with respect to the port canal , as "Lidi Nord" or "Lidi Sud".
